Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

1. Overview

SCS Play ("the App") is developed by Messkraft. This policy describes how we handle your data.

2. Data We Collect

The App stores game progress (scores, achievements, settings) locally on your device using your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly sign in with an account.

3. Optional Account

If you choose to create an account via Firebase Authentication, we store:

  • Your email address (for login purposes only)
  • Game progress data (scores, XP, achievements) synced to Firebase Firestore

Account creation is entirely optional. The App is fully functional without an account.

4. Third-Party Services

The App may use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication — for optional account management
  • Firebase Firestore — for optional cloud sync of game data

These services are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Advertising

The App does not display ads during gameplay. If rewarded ads are offered, they are opt-in only and handled through standard ad SDKs with their own privacy policies.

6. Analytics

The App does not use analytics or tracking services. No usage data is sent to external servers.

7. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us.

8. Data Deletion

You can delete all local data by clearing your browser's storage. If you have an account, you can request account and data deletion by contacting us.

9. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

10. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries: privacy@messkraft.com
